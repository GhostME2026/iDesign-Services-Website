Before You Message
How much will it cost?

Every job is priced based on what it actually involves, so I don't give flat prices upfront. Send a message with what you need and I'll quote you clearly before any work starts.

How long does it take?

It depends on the service — design work is often same-day, while registrations and official applications depend on government processing times outside my control. I'll always give you a realistic estimate upfront.

Do you handle the official submission yourself?

For passport, birth certificate, and recruitment applications, I help you prepare and correctly complete everything required — forms, documents, and appointment booking. The official process itself is always handled through the relevant government office.

Can you print and deliver, or just design?

Both — flyers, banners, brochures, and certificates can be designed and printed, not just sent to you as a file.

Do you only work in person?

No. Technical support, website work, cybersecurity teaching, and most design work can be done fully remotely. Installations (CCTV, dash cam) and printing naturally need to happen in person.
